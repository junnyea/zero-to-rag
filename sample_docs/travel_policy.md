# Acme Corp Corporate Travel & Expense Policy

**Document ID:** ACME-FIN-TRV-2026  
**Effective Date:** February 1, 2026  
**Version:** 2.5  
**Owner:** Finance and Accounts Payable Team  

---

## 1. Flight Booking and Class of Service
To optimize corporate spending, all business-related air travel must be booked through our approved booking system.

*   **Official Portal:** All flights must be booked using the **AcmeTravel online portal** (accessible at `https://travel.acme.corp`). Direct bookings with airlines will not be reimbursed without prior written VP-level approval.
*   **Booking Lead Time:** Domestic flights must be booked at least **14 days in advance**. International flights must be booked at least **21 days in advance**.
*   **Class of Service:** 
    *   **Economy Class:** Mandatory for all flights with a continuous flight duration of **under 6 hours**.
    *   **Premium Economy / Business Class:** Allowed for international flights with a continuous flight duration of **6 hours or more**, subject to department head sign-off. First-class travel is never reimbursable.

---

## 2. Lodging Guidelines and Allowable Limits
Acme Corp reimburses reasonable hotel accommodation costs for business travel.

*   **Standard Lodging Limit:** The maximum allowable nightly rate for standard business travel is **$180 per night** (excluding local taxes).
*   **High-Cost City Exceptions:** For travel to designated high-cost urban centers, the maximum allowable nightly rate is increased to **$300 per night** (excluding taxes). Designated high-cost cities are:
    *   New York City (NYC), USA
    *   San Francisco, USA
    *   London, UK
    *   Tokyo, Japan
    *   Zurich, Switzerland
*   **Booking Requirement:** Hotels must be booked through the AcmeTravel portal. Ancillary costs (minibar, spa, in-room movies) are strictly personal expenses and will not be reimbursed.

---

## 3. Daily Meal Allowance (Per Diem)
Employees traveling on official company business are provided a daily meal allowance.

*   **Daily Max Limit:** The maximum reimbursable daily meal expense is **$80 per day** total.
*   **Meal Allocation (Suggested Cap):**
    *   **Breakfast:** Up to **$15**
    *   **Lunch:** Up to **$25**
    *   **Dinner:** Up to **$40**
*   **Receipt Requirement:** Detailed, itemized receipts (not just credit card slips) are required for any single meal expense exceeding **$25**. 
*   **Alcohol Policy:** Reasonable expense for alcohol (e.g., one glass of wine or beer with dinner) is reimbursable if consumed during a business meal, but cannot exceed the daily $80 limit.

---

## 4. Expense Report Submission and Deadlines
Reimbursement for out-of-pocket expenses incurred during business travel is processed through the Finance department.

*   **Submission Window:** Expense reports, along with scanned copies of itemized receipts, must be submitted in the Acme Expense portal within **30 calendar days** of returning from the trip.
*   **Late Submission Penalty:** Expense reports submitted more than **45 days** after the trip's completion are subject to rejection, and the expenses may not be reimbursed.
*   **Processing Time:** Approved expense reports are processed within **10 business days** of submission, and reimbursements are disbursed via direct deposit.
