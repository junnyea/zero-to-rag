## 1. Core Working Hours and Office Schedule
Acme Corp operates on a flexible-hybrid work model. The standard core hours of operation during which all full-time employees are expected to be available are **10:00 AM to 4:00 PM EST**. 

The standard weekly commitment is 40 hours. Individual daily schedules may be adjusted around these core hours (for example, working 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM or 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM), subject to manager approval.

---

## 2. Probationary Period
All newly hired full-time employees undergo an initial probationary period of **90 calendar days**, starting on their first day of employment. 

At the 45-day and 90-day marks, managers will conduct formal performance reviews. Successful completion of the probationary period will be confirmed in writing by the HR Department.

---

## 3. Paid Time Off (PTO) and Leaves
Acme Corp believes in work-life balance and provides generous paid leave options to all eligible employees.

### 3.1 Annual Leave (Vacation)
Full-time employees accrue vacation days monthly, totaling **22 paid days per calendar year**. 
*   A maximum of **5 unused vacation days** can be rolled over to the next calendar year. Any additional unused days beyond 5 will be forfeited on December 31st.
*   Vacation requests of 3 or more consecutive days must be submitted to managers at least **2 weeks in advance**.

### 3.2 Parental Leave
Acme Corp supports growing families. All primary and secondary caregivers are eligible for parental leave:
*   **Primary Caregiver Leave:** Up to **16 weeks** of fully paid parental leave following the birth, adoption, or foster placement of a child.
*   **Secondary Caregiver Leave:** Up to **6 weeks** of fully paid parental leave.
*   This leave must be taken within **12 months** of the qualifying birth or placement event.

## 4. Employee Health & Wellness Benefits
Acme Corp offers comprehensive medical, dental, and vision coverage through three premium plan tiers:

1.  **Acme Alpha Plan (PPO):**
    *   Low deductible ($500 individual / $1,000 family).
    *   Co-insurance of 10% after deductible is met.
    *   Best for families and frequent healthcare users.
2.  **Acme Beta Plan (EPO):**
    *   Moderate deductible ($1,200 individual / $2,400 family).
    *   In-network coverage only (no out-of-network benefits).
    *   Co-insurance of 20% after deductible is met.
3.  **Acme Delta Plan (HDHP + HSA):**
    *   High deductible ($3,000 individual / $6,000 family).
    *   100% preventive care coverage.
    *   Includes a company contribution of **$1,000 annually** to the employee's Health Savings Account (HSA).

## 5. Dress Code Guideline
Our office environment is **Smart Casual**. Employees are expected to dress professionally but comfortably. 
*   **Permitted:** Dark jeans without rips, chinos, blouses, button-down shirts, polo shirts, sweaters, clean sneakers, loafers, and flats.
*   **Prohibited:** Clothing containing offensive graphics, athletic sweatpants, swimwear, flip-flops, torn/ripped jeans, and beachwear.
*   **Client Meetings:** Business formal attire (suits, blazers, dress pants, dresses) is required when representing Acme Corp in client-facing environments.
