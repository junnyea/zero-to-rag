# Acme Corp IT Security & Acceptable Use Policy

**Document ID:** ACME-IT-SEC-2026  
**Effective Date:** March 15, 2026  
**Version:** 4.1  
**Owner:** IT Security and Infrastructure Team  

---

## 1. Password Complexity and Rotation Guidelines
Securing company accounts is critical to protecting our infrastructure. All employees must adhere to the following password rules:

*   **Minimum Length:** All passwords must be at least **15 characters** in length.
*   **Composition Requirements:** Passwords must contain a mixture of:
    *   At least **one uppercase letter** (A-Z)
    *   At least **one lowercase letter** (a-z)
    *   At least **one number** (0-9)
    *   At least **one special character** (e.g., `!`, `@`, `#`, `$`, `%`, `^`, `*`)
*   **Rotation Frequency:** Passwords must be changed every **90 calendar days**. The identity provider will prompt you 14 days before expiration.
*   **History Constraint:** You cannot reuse any of your last **8 passwords**.

---

## 2. Remote Work and Secure Connections (VPN)
All remote work must be conducted securely. When working from outside an Acme Corp physical office (home, coffee shop, hotel, etc.):

*   **VPN Requirement:** Employees must connect to the corporate network via the **Acme SecureVPN** client. Direct access to internal portals without VPN is blocked.
*   **Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA):** Every VPN connection and system login requires validation via the **Duo Mobile app** on your registered company smartphone. 
*   **Public Wi-Fi:** Working on unsecured public Wi-Fi networks (without encryption or passwords) is strictly prohibited unless connected to Acme SecureVPN first.

---

## 3. Incident and Phishing Response Procedure
Phishing is the single largest threat to our corporate security. 

*   **Suspicious Emails:** If you receive an email that looks suspicious, contains unexpected attachments, requests credential entry, or asks for urgent wire transfers, do not click any links or download any files.
*   **Reporting Address:** Forward the suspicious email as an attachment immediately to **phish@acme.corp**.
*   **What to Include:** Use the "Phish Alert" button in Microsoft Outlook, or manually forward it with the subject line `SUSPICIOUS EMAIL - [Your Department]`.
*   **Accidental Clicks:** If you accidentally click a link or enter your password on an external page, report the incident immediately by calling the IT Emergency Hotline at **(555) 019-9999** so your account can be locked and scanned.

---

## 4. Hardware Security and Encryption
All corporate laptops are issued with security measures pre-installed.

*   **BitLocker Encryption:** All internal storage drives are encrypted with BitLocker. Tampering with or disabling BitLocker is a violation of this policy.
*   **Auto-Lock Timeout:** Laptops are configured to lock automatically after **5 minutes of inactivity**. Employees must manually lock their screens (`Win + L` on Windows, `Ctrl + Cmd + Q` on Mac) whenever they step away from their desk.
*   **Personal Devices:** Accessing corporate resources (email, Slack, GitHub) from personal laptops or tablets is prohibited unless specifically authorized via the Bring Your Own Device (BYOD) registry.
