# ACME Corp Remote Work and Telecommuting Policy

This document establishes the guidelines, eligibility, and expectations for employees working remotely or from home.

## 1. Hybrid Work Model
ACME Corp operates on a 3:2 hybrid work model. All employees are expected to work from an ACME physical office on Tuesdays, Wednesdays, and Thursdays. Mondays and Fridays are designated as optional remote work days, where employees may choose to work from home or another suitable location.

## 2. Core Working Hours
Regardless of location, all employees must be available and online during our core working hours of 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM Eastern Time. These hours are designed to facilitate cross-team collaboration, synchronous meetings, and timely communications.

## 3. Home Office Equipment Stipend
To support comfortable and ergonomic remote work setups, ACME Corp offers a one-time home office equipment stipend of up to $500. This stipend can be claimed for items such as ergonomic office chairs, monitors, keyboards, mice, or desks. Claims must be submitted with valid receipts through the expense portal.

## 4. Internet and Connectivity Requirements
Employees working remotely must ensure they have a stable, secure, and fast internet connection with a minimum download speed of 25 Mbps and upload speed of 10 Mbps. This is necessary to participate effectively in video conferences and run company software without disruption.
