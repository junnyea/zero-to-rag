## 1. Where is the Acme Corp headquarters located?
Our corporate headquarters address is:
**Acme Corp Headquarters**  
**789 Innovation Boulevard, Suite 500**  
**Tech City, CA 94016**  

The office is situated on the 5th floor of the Innovation Towers building. Visitors can check in at the main reception desk in the ground-floor lobby.

---

## 2. What are the rules for parking at the headquarters?
Acme Corp provides parking facilities for both staff and visitors:

*   **Employee Parking:** Employees must register their vehicles with Facilities to receive a parking decal. Registered employee parking is restricted to **Lot C** (located behind the main building). Parking in other lots without authorization may result in towing.
*   **Visitor Parking:** Visitors and clients can park in **Lot A** (directly in front of the lobby entrance). Visitor parking is free for the first 2 hours. For stays exceeding 2 hours, visitors must obtain a validation stamp from the 5th-floor reception desk.
*   **Bicycle Storage:** Secure bicycle racks and lockers are located on Level 1 of the parking garage, adjacent to Lot C.

## 4. What is the office kitchen policy, and when are snacks restocked?
We are proud to offer a fully stocked micro-kitchen on the 5th floor for all employees.

*   **Restocking Schedule:** Fresh fruits, snacks, espresso beans, cold brew kegs, and beverages are restocked twice a week: **every Monday morning at 8:00 AM** and **every Thursday morning at 8:00 AM**.
*   **Cleanliness Rules:** Employees are responsible for loading their used dishes and mugs into the office dishwashers. The refrigerator is cleared out completely **every Friday evening at 5:00 PM**. Any containers, food, or drinks left in the fridge at this time will be discarded—no exceptions.
*   **Dietary Needs:** Standard provisions include gluten-free, vegan, and nut-free options. Special dietary requests can be submitted to the Operations Slack channel (`#ops-facilities`).

---

## 5. Which holidays is the office closed for?
Acme Corp physical offices are closed on the following public holidays:
*   New Year's Day (January 1st)
*   Memorial Day (Last Monday in May)
*   Independence Day (July 4th)
*   Labor Day (First Monday in September)
*   Thanksgiving Day & Black Friday (Fourth Thursday and Friday in November)
*   Christmas Eve & Christmas Day (December 24th and 25th)

*Note:* If a holiday falls on a Saturday, it is observed on the preceding Friday. If it falls on a Sunday, it is observed on the following Monday.
